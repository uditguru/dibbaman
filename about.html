Our story, our value

In our busy lives, we never think what we are eating. We just go to the workplace, and in our lunch time we eat food that might be junk or with nil protein value. It is observed that the food which is provided in office or in college canteens is not having any of the three main characteristics of a good meal- taste, hygiene and required protein value. One cannot deny that home food is something that we all want in our offices' lunch time. So, here we are, taking responsibility to provide you the food you really need in your lunch/dinner time while you are in your workplace. We are here to serve you hot & freshly cooked lunch made by your home ministers. All of this is possible without waking them early in the morning. Now, why you need our service. 1) Think about your mother or wife, you are waking them up at 5 in the morning just for preparing your lunch. After completing so many household activities they also deserve a good night's sleep. 2) Since they are preparing it so early morning, therefore food does not remain fresh & hot by the time you eat it. 3) Why waste money in canteens or in tiffin centres when you can easily get your home made food. 4) Anyone could take benefit of our services , from school kids to techies; all of you can easily access our services. 5) Forgot taking your lunchbox to the workplace ?
